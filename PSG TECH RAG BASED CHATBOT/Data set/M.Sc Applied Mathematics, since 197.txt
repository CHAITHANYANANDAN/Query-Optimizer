Programme Coordinator : Dr.Sai Sundara Krishnan G


The objective of the Master of Science programme in Applied Mathematics is to provide the students with expertise in advanced mathematics along with its applications in computer science. This ensures the students to gain knowledge of mathematical and computational skills. The course provides hands on training in various mathematical tools and computer software related to their career direction. The department provides a smooth research environment by providing opportunities to work in various research domains. Students are exposed to industrial environment through a solid six months project in various academic and software industries.
