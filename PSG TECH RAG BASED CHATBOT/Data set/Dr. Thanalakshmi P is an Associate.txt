Dr. Thanalakshmi P is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She joined the department on 02/07/2008. Dr. Thanalakshmi completed her Ph.D in Mathematics from Anna University on 25/09/2020, under the supervision of Dr. R. Anitha. She holds an M.Sc in Mathematics from Manonmaniam Sundaranar University (1995) and an M.Phil in Mathematics from Madurai Kamaraj University (1996). She also holds PGDCA and PGDOR certifications.

Dr. Thanalakshmi is passionate about inspiring students towards academic excellence. Her areas of expertise include Applied Linear Algebra, Calculus and Its Applications, Computational Number Theory, Cryptography, Differential Equations, Discrete Structures, Graph Theory and Its Applications, Linear Algebra and Fourier Series, and Probability and Statistics. Her research interests primarily focus on Cryptography.

She can be contacted at ptl.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
