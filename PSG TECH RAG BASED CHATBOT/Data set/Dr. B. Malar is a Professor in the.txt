Dr. B. Malar is a Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology. She joined the institution on 17/06/2005 and has 17 years of teaching experience and 10 years of research experience. She completed her B.C.A (1997) and M.C.A (2000) from Bharathiar University, and her Ph.D in Computer Science (07/12/2015) from Anna University, under the supervision of Dr. R. Nadarajan. She also obtained her M.Phil in Computer Science (07/12/2015) from Bharathiar University, under the supervision of Dr. K. Vivekanandan. Her subject expertise includes Advanced Data Structures, Data Structures, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Compiler Design, and Data Mining. Her research interests focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing. Currently, she is working on Feature Engineering and Deep Learning for NLP Applications. She can be contacted at bmr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
