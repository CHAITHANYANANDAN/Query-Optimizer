Dr. Janani K is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 19/07/2024. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2018) from PSG College of Arts and Science, an M.Sc in Mathematics (2020) from Bharathiar University, and a Ph.D in Mathematics (2024) from Bharathiar University, supervised by Dr. R. Rakkiyappan. Her research areas include Machine Learning, Fuzzy Sets and Systems, and Differential Equations. She can be contacted at kja.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
