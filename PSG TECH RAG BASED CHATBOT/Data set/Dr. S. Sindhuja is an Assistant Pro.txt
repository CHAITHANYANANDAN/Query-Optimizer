Dr. S. Sindhuja is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology. She joined the institution on 06/03/2023. She earned her B.Sc in Computer Science (2009) from Dr. SNS Rajalakshmi College of Arts and Science, her M.C.A (2012) from PSG College of Arts and Science, and completed her Ph.D in Computer Science on 28/09/2021 from Bharathiar University, under the supervision of Dr. R. Vadivel. Her subject expertise includes Intrusion Detection Systems, Computer Networks, and Data Structures and Algorithms. Her research interests span Advanced Networking, Wireless Sensor Networks, and Mobile Ad Hoc Networks (MANET). She can be contacted at ssd.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
