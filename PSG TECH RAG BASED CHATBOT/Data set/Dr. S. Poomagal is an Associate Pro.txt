Dr. S. Poomagal is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science with 18 years of teaching experience. She joined PSG College of Technology on 01/06/2007. She holds a B.Sc in Applied Sciences (2001) and an M.Sc in Computer Technology (2003) from Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, Sathyamangalam, and completed her Ph.D in Computer Science on 04/04/2014 from PSG College of Technology under the supervision of Dr. P. Visalakshi. Her subject expertise includes C Programming, Data Mining, Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing, Data Structures, and Design and Analysis of Algorithms. Her research interests are in Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Sentiment Analysis. You can contact her via spm.amcs@psgtech.ac.in
