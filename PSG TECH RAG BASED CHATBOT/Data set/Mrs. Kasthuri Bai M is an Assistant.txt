Mrs. Kasthuri Bai M is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 01/06/2007. She holds a B.Sc in Computer Science (1992) from Government College of Technology, Bharathiyar University, Coimbatore, an M.C.A (1995) from Seethalakshmi Ramaswamy College, Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli, and an M.Phil in Computer Science (2007) from Periyar University. Mrs. Kasthuri Bai has over 15 years of academic experience and has mentored more than 100 undergraduate and postgraduate students on their projects. She is keen on learning new programming languages and technologies to solve problems. Her subject expertise includes NoSQL Databases and Database Management Systems. Mrs. Kasthuri Bai can be contacted at mkb.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
