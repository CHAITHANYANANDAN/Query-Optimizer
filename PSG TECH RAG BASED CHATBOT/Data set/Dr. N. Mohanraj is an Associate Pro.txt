Dr. N. Mohanraj is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology. He joined the institution on 13/06/2022 and has over 20 years of academic experience along with 9 years of industrial experience. He completed his B.Sc in Physics (1986) from Kongunadu Arts & Science College, his M.C.A in Computer Applications (1989) from PSG College of Technology, and a PGDBA in 2001. He earned his Ph.D in Computer Science (07/11/2016) from Anna University. His research interests focus on Agile Software Development and Software Engineering. Dr. Mohanraj is currently serving as the Department Alumni Coordinator. His subject expertise includes Object-Oriented Modelling and Design, Software Engineering, Software Testing and Quality Assurance, Software Project Management and Quality Assurance. His research area is Agile Modeling. He can be contacted at nmr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
