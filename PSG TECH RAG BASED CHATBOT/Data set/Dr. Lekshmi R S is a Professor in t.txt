Dr. Lekshmi R S is a Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 06/11/1997. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (1991) from Women's Christian College, Madurai Kamaraj University, an M.Sc in Mathematics (1993) from Scott Christian College, Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, and a Ph.D in Mathematics (2009) from Bharathiar University, supervised by Dr. R. Anitha. Additionally, she completed her M.Phil in Mathematics (1994) from Alagappa University, Karaikudi. With 23 years of teaching experience, her research areas include Graph Theory and Graph Algorithms. She teaches Graph Theory and is available for Ph.D. registration in this field (Anna University). She can be contacted at rs.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
