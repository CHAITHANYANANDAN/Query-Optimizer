Dr. Regis Anne W is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She joined the department on 24/04/2021. Dr. Regis Anne earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Bharathiar University on 16/10/2017, under the supervision of Dr. Elijah Blessing. Her doctoral research focused on Wireless Sensor Networks.

She has over 17 years of teaching experience and has published papers in several reputed national and international journals. Additionally, Dr. Regis Anne serves as a reviewer for various International Journals. Her current research interests include Deep Learning and Data Analytics.

Her subject expertise includes C and Java Programming, Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Communication Networks, and Object-Oriented Programming.

You can contact her at wra.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
