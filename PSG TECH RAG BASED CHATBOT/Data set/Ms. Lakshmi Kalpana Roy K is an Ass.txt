Ms. Lakshmi Kalpana Roy K is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 01/06/2023. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2000) and an M.Sc in Information Technology (2002), both from The Gandhigram Rural Institute, Deemed University. With over 19 years of teaching experience, she currently teaches Social Network Data Analytics. Her subject expertise includes Social Network Analysis and Object-Oriented Programming, while her research area is Computational Neuroscience. She can be contacted at lkr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
