Dr. Caroline Mary A is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She joined the department on 01/06/2023. Dr. Caroline Mary completed her B.Sc. in Information Technology and M.Sc. in Computer Technology from PSG College of Technology. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Hindusthan College of Arts and Science, with research in the area of Networks, under the supervision of Dr. A V Senthil Kumar.

Dr. Caroline Mary has cleared the NET and SET exams and has expertise in C Programming, Cryptography and Network Security, Machine Learning, and Operating Systems. Her current research focus is on Networks.
