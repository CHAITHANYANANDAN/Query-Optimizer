Mr. S. Krishnakumar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, with over 12 years of teaching experience. He joined the institution on 05/04/2023. He completed his B.Sc in Physics (2005) from Loyola College, Madras University, his M.C.A (2008) from Vellore Institute of Technology, and his M.E in Computer Science (2014) from K. Ramakrishnan College of Technology, Anna University. He has cleared the TN-SET examination in 2012 and the NET examination in 2019. His subject expertise includes Operating Systems, Web Programming, Computer Architecture, and Software Engineering. His research interests include Machine Learning and Deep Learning. He can be contacted at skk.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
