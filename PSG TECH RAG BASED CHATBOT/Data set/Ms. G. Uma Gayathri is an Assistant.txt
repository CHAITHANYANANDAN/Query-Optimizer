Ms. G. Uma Gayathri is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology. She joined the institution on 12/12/2022. She completed her B.Sc in Mathematics with Computer Applications (2019) from Madurai Kamaraj University and her M.Sc in Applied Mathematics (2021) from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She is currently pursuing her Ph.D in Mathematics at PSG College of Technology, under the supervision of Dr. N. K. Sreeja. Her subject expertise includes Object Oriented Programming and Computational Number Theory and Cryptography. Her research interests lie in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. She can be contacted at umg.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
