Dr. Megala M is an Assistant Professor (Sl. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She joined the institution on 13/09/2006 and has over 15 years of teaching experience. She holds a B.Sc in Home Science (1999) from Avinashilingam University, an M.C.A (2002) from Bharathiar University, an M.Phil in Computer Science from Bharathidasan University, and a Ph.D in Computer Science (23/07/2018) from Anna University, supervised by Dr. G. Umamaheswari. Her subject expertise includes Computer Architecture, Advanced Computer Architecture, Database Management Systems, Microprocessor and Interfacing, Microprocessor and Microcontrollers, Web Security, and Java Programming. Her research area is focused on Web Security. She can be contacted at mm.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
