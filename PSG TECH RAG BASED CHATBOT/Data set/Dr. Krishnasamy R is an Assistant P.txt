Dr. Krishnasamy R is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, having joined in January 2023. He previously served as an Assistant Professor at the same institution from 2019 to 2022. Dr. Krishnasamy completed his B.Sc in Mathematics (2006) from Sri Vasavi College, M.Sc in Mathematics (2008) from Bharathiar University, and M.Phil in Mathematics (2009) from Gandhigram Rural University, under the supervision of Prof. P. Balasubramaniam. He earned his Ph.D. in Mathematics (2014) from The Gandhigram Rural Institute - Deemed University, also under the supervision of Prof. P. Balasubramaniam. Additionally, he completed Post-Doctoral Research under Prof. Raju K George at the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Thiruvananthapuram, sponsored by DST-SERB from June 2017 to May 2019. His research focuses on stability and stabilizability of dynamical systems, particularly neutral systems, singular systems, and stochastic systems. Dr. Krishnasamy is a member of the Indian Society for Technical Education and has published 15 research papers and two book chapters. He is a recognized research supervisor in Anna University for guiding Ph.D. candidates. His subject expertise includes Linear Algebra, Differential Equations, Abstract Algebra, Calculus, Optimization Techniques, Discrete Structures, and Simulation and Modeling Techniques. He can be contacted at rky.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
