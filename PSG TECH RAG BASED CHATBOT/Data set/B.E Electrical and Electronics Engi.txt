The department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering has been for long, a pillar of strength in PSG College of Technology and also in engineering Community, since 1951. Our department offers under graduate programmes in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and sandwich EEE. It also offers two post graduate programmes- Embedded & Real-Time Systems and Power Electronics & Drives . The department is the center for quality improvement programmes (QIP) for Post Graduate and Doctoral Programmes. It has highly qualified faculty and equipped with excellent lab facilities.
