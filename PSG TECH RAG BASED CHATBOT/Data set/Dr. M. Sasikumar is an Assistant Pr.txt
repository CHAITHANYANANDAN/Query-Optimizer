Dr. M. Sasikumar is an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) in the Department of Computer Science with 22 years of teaching experience. He joined PSG College of Technology on 01/06/2011. He completed his B.Sc in Home Science (1993) from Sengunthar Arts and Science College, Tiruchengode, Madras University, followed by an M.C.A (1996) from Erode Arts College, Erode, Bharathiar University. He also holds an M.Phil in Computer Science (01/11/2003) from Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, supervised by Mr. R. Shanmugasundaram, and earned his Ph.D in Computer Science (22/05/2015) from Anna University, under the supervision of Dr. R. Anitha. Dr. Sasikumar qualified the SET examination in October 2012 and the NET examination in June 2020. His subject expertise includes Computer Networks, Mobile Computing, Web Programming, Data Communication Networks, TCP/IP Networks and Applications, Web Technologies, Web Services, Object Oriented Programming, and XML and its Applications. His research area focuses on Wireless Sensor Networks. He can be contacted at msr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
