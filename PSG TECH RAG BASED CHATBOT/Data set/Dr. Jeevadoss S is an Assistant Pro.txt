Dr. Jeevadoss S is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, having joined on 01/06/2016. He holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2006) and an M.Sc in Mathematics (2008) from Bishop Heber College, Tiruchirappalli, an M.Phil in Mathematics (2010) from Kongunadu Arts and Science College, Coimbatore, and a Ph.D in Mathematics (2015) from Periyar University, supervised by Dr. Muthusamy A. His subject expertise includes Discrete Structures, Graph Theory and its Applications, Calculus and its Applications, Discrete Mathematics, and Linear Algebra. His research area focuses on Graph Theory and its Applications. He is a recognized Ph.D. supervisor at Anna University and has been a Mathematical Reviewer for the American Mathematical Society and Zentralblatt MATH Reviewer for the European Mathematical Society. He qualified the Tamil Nadu State Eligibility Test (SET) in February 2016 for Lectureship in Mathematical Sciences. He can be contacted at sjd.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
