Dr. M. Shakthiganesan is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 01/06/2018. He holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2010) and an M.Sc in Mathematics (2012), both from SBK College, and a Ph.D in Mathematics from SBK College, under the supervision of Dr. R. Vembu. Dr. Shakthiganesan cleared both the National Eligibility Test for Lecturership (NET) and the State Eligibility Test (SET) in the general category. His research area focuses on Topological Structures and Fixed Point Theory. His subject expertise includes Linear Algebra, Topology, and Fuzzy Sets and Systems. He can be contacted at mst.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
