Ms. Abilasha P B is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 01/06/2023. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2019) and an M.Sc in Mathematics (2021) from PSG College of Arts and Science, and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Mathematics at PSG College of Technology, under the supervision of Dr. C Veeramani. Her subject expertise includes Differential Equations and Calculus and Its Applications, with a research focus on Epidemic Models. She can be contacted at abs.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
