Dr. Sridevi UK is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 19/06/2019. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (1998) and an M.C.A (2001) from Bharathiar University, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science (2012) from Anna University, supervised by Dr. N. Nagaveni. Dr. Sridevi has over 18 years of teaching experience and her research interests include Information Retrieval, Data Mining, and Data Analytics. She has published around 15 papers in various journals, book chapters, and international/national conference proceedings. She is a recognized Research Supervisor in Anna University, Chennai. Her subject expertise covers Big Data Analytics, Data Mining, Predictive Analytics, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. Dr. Sridevi can be contacted at uks.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
