Mrs. S. Deepa is an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, India, with over 15 years of academic experience. She joined the institution on 11/09/2006. She obtained her B.Sc in Computer Science (2000), M.C.A (2003), and M.Phil in Computer Science (27/05/2009) from Bharathiar University, under the supervision of Dr. R. Vijayalakshmi. Additionally, she has completed an IBM Mainframe certificate course. Mrs. Deepa has guided more than 50 B.Sc (CT, IT, and CSD) and M.Sc (CT) projects. Her subject expertise includes Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Microprocessor and Interfacing, Web Programming, Microprocessor and Microcontrollers, Open Source Systems, and Python. Her research interests lie in the areas of Wireless Networks, Network Analysis, Web Mining, and Machine Learning. She can be contacted at nsd.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
