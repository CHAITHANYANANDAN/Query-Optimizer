Dr. Sreelaja N K is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 07/06/2017. She holds a B.Sc in Physics (1996) from Bharathiar University, an M.C.A (1999) from Avinashilingam University, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science (2012) from Anna University, supervised by Dr. G.A. Vijayalakshmi Pai. Dr. Sreelaja has more than 18 years of teaching experience and her research interests include Cryptography, Security in Computing, Computational Intelligence, and Social Network Analysis. She has published 15 papers in various journals, edited book chapters, and international/national conference proceedings. She is a recognized Research Supervisor in Anna University, Chennai. Her subject expertise covers Database Management Systems, Computer Forensics, Information Security, Unix System Programming, and Security in Computing. Dr. Sreelaja can be contacted at nkr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
