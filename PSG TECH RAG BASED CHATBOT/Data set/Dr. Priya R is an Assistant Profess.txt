Dr. Priya R is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. She joined the department on 18/11/2011. Dr. Priya completed her M.Phil. in Network Security from PSG College of Arts and Science in 2010. She holds both M.Sc. (Computer Technology) and B.Sc. (Computer Technology) degrees from PSG College of Technology, obtained in 2009 and 2007, respectively. Dr. Priya earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Anna University on 14/08/2020, under the supervision of Dr. R. Vidhyapriya.

Her research interests include Data Security, Image and Video Processing, Cryptography and Network Security, and Operating Systems. She has published several papers in reputed journals.

Her expertise spans Information Security, Cryptography and Network Security, and Database Management Systems (DBMS).

You can contact her at rpr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
