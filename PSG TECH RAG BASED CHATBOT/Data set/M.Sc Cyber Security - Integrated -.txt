The five year integrated M.Sc. Cyber Security programme introduced in the year 2020, the first of its kind in India aims to prepare students with the technical knowledge and skills needed to protect and defend computer systems and networks. The programme has a strong and wide technical base and internship programs which are the most critical aspects to a good cybersecurity education.
