Mr. Gowrisankar S is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, having joined on 01/07/2022. He holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (CA) (2006) from Kaamadhenu Arts and Science College, an M.Sc in Mathematics (2008) from Bharathiar University, an M.Phil in Mathematics (2010) from Bharathidasan University, and is pursuing a Ph.D in Mathematics at PSG College of Technology, supervised by Dr. R. Krishnasamy. His subject expertise includes Linear Algebra, Optimization Techniques, Calculus and Its Applications, Differential Equations, and Applied Geometry. His research area focuses on Differential Equations, Complex Networked Systems, and Stability Analysis. He has qualified the SET Exam (2012) and the GATE Exam (2012). He can be contacted at sgs.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
