Dr. Aparna V is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 14/06/2023. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2015) from St. Teresa's College, Kochi, an M.Sc in Mathematics (2017) from Sacred Heart College, Kochi, and a Ph.D. in Mathematics from Bharathiar University (2023), under the supervision of Dr. N. Mohanapriya. She has also qualified GATE (2019). Her subject expertise includes Linear Algebra and Graph Theory, with a research focus on Graph Theory and its Applications. She has published papers in International Journals. She can be contacted at apr.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
