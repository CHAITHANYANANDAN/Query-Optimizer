Mrs. Parisa Begum K is an Assistant Professor (Sr. Gr.) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, having joined on 01/06/2009. She holds a B.Sc in Computer Science (1992) from Bharathiar University, an M.C.A (2007) from Anna University, and a Ph.D in Computer Science from Anna University, supervised by Dr. Shina Sheen. Her subject expertise includes Computer Graphics and Multimedia, Sentiment Analysis, Object Oriented Programming, Computer Vision, C and Java Programming, and Web Programming. Her research area focuses on Data Management in Pervasive Computing Environment. She can be contacted at kpb.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
