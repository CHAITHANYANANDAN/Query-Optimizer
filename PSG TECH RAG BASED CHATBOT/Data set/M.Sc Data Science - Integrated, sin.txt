M.Sc Data Science - Integrated, since 2015
Programme Coordinator : Dr.Senthil Kumar M


The Five year integrated M.Sc. Data Science is an innovative data analysis programme introduced by PSG College of Technology introduced during 2015 to meet the challenges of Data driven analysis and Big data. This course will equip the students with necessary skills in Mathematics, Statistics and Computer Science to handle the challenges of Big data analysis. The need for data scientists is predicted to have a sharp growth in coming years. M.Sc Data science course aims to meet these new demands. Like the other two five year integrated programmes this course also have two internships, one in seventh semester and the other in tenth semester.
