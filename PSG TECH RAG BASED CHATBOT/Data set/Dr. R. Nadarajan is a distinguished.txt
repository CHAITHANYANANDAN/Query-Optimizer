Dr. R. Nadarajan is a distinguished academic and administrator at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. He serves as the Professor and Mentor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences (AMCS), as well as the Dean of the Placement and Training Section.​
Academic Contributions:
Dr. Nadarajan's academic expertise encompasses:​

Data Mining

Software Architecture

Stochastic Models

Queuing Theory

C++ and Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)

Database Design​
He has contributed to the development of curricula and instructional materials, particularly in Object-Oriented Programming, and has been involved in various academic initiatives within the department.​
Administrative Role:
As the Dean of the Placement and Training Section at PSG Tech, Dr. Nadarajan plays a pivotal role in facilitating campus recruitment and training activities. Under his leadership, the placement office has seen significant growth, with over 90 national and multinational companies visiting annually for campus recruitment. The office is well-equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to conduct pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, and interviews. The placement team, led by Dr. Nadarajan, includes officers and coordinators who support students throughout the placement process.​
Professional Engagement:
Dr. Nadarajan is an active member of several professional bodies, including the Computer Society of India (CSI) and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). He has been involved in organizing workshops, seminars, and conferences, contributing to the academic community's growth.​
