Robotics and Automation is a unique interdisciplinary programme that provides in-depth knowledge in the areas of Robot Kinematics and Dynamics, PLC & SCADA, Special Application Sensors, Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Artificial Intelligence, etc. To better reflect its interdisciplinary nature the programme covers designing Robots for dedicated applications, maintaining them, developing new concepts, and doing research in the Automation system. This programme in Robotics and Automation Engineering focuses on gear the budding graduates as Proficient Engineers with an enduring background in Science and Engineering.
