Dr. Suresh Kumar V is an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 01/06/2007. He holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (2002) and M.Sc in Mathematics (2004) from C.B.M College/Bharathiar University, an M.Phil in Mathematics (2005) from Alagappa University, and a Ph.D in Mathematics from PSG College of Technology/Anna University in 2017, with a thesis titled "Design and Analysis of Security Protocols using Formal Methods" under the supervision of Dr. R. Anitha. His research interests include Electronic Voting Protocols, Electronic Payment Protocols, Authentication and Key Establishment Protocols for RFID and WSN, Security Modelling and Analysis, and Formal Methods. He has published extensively in peer-reviewed international journals and conferences and has served as a reviewer for journals such as IEEE Systems Journal, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics, IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing, IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Computing, IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, IEEE Sensors Journal, ACM Transactions on Internet Technology, IET Communications, and Wiley publishers. He is a recognized supervisor for PhD/MS research scholars at Anna University (Ref. No. 3170022). He can be reached at sand.amcs@psgtech.ac.in.
