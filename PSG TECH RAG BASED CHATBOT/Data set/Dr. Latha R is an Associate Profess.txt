Dr. Latha R is an Associate Professor (SG) in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences at PSG College of Technology, having joined on 16/06/2006. She holds a B.Sc in Mathematics (1989) from PSGR Krishnammal College for Women, Bharathiar University, an M.Sc in Applied Mathematics (1991) from PSG College of Technology, Bharathiar University, an M.C.A (2000), and a Ph.D. in Computer Science (2017) from Anna University, under the supervision of Dr. R. Nadarajan. She also completed her M.Phil in Computer Science (2017) from Bharathiar University, supervised by Dr. Bhuvaneshwari K. Dr. Latha has over 17 years of teaching experience, with expertise in Recommender Systems, Information Retrieval, Machine Learning, Data Privacy, and Manifold Learning. Her doctoral thesis focused on Collaborative Recommender Systems with User Preference Mining Approaches. She is a member of the Computer Society of India (CSI) and ACM.
